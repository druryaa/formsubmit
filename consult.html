Consultation Process

The following documents are helpful during the inital consultation:

(1) Complete intake form

(2) A copy of your last 3 years of notices of assessment

(3) A recent pay stub

(4) Any correspondence serve on you by the opposing party or their lawyer

Completed Intake Form

An intake form can be completed online here: Client Information Form

Notices of Assessment

In order to determine the support obligations of either party, historic tax returns from the last three years is helpful. Without accurate information concerning income, any questions concerning spousal support obligations, child support, contributions to expenses for children, and retroactive child support, may be difficult to answer. It is helpful to bring any income tax infomration you have to your consultation in order to have a more effective initial meeting.

Recent Pay Stub

A recent pay stub is helpful in determining questions about prospective support obligations Moreover, a recent pay stub will have valuable information concerning helth benefits, and pension contributions that may be relevant to your matter.

Correpondence

If your former spouse has retained counsel, and you have been served with a letter or pleadings, make sure to bring all documents to your consultation.
